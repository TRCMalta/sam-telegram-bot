# Data Protection Policy

TRC's internal policy on personal data, aligned with the General Data Protection Regulation and the Maltese Data Protection Act.

## Principles ([[GDPR Article 30 - ROPA|Article 5 GDPR]])

TRC processes personal data:

1. **Lawfully, fairly, and transparently.**
2. For **specified, explicit and legitimate purposes** — no purpose creep.
3. **Minimised** to what is necessary.
4. **Accurate** and kept up to date.
5. **Retained no longer than necessary** — see `Records Retention Schedule.md`.
6. With **integrity and confidentiality** — see § Technical and organisational measures below.
7. **Accountably** — TRC documents this in the vault.

## Roles

- **[[Data Controller]]:** TRC — represented by the Governor.
- **[[DPO]]:** Assessment in `DPO Assessment.md`.
- **Asset owners:** named in each asset note.

## Lawful basis

The default lawful basis for processing TRC operational personal data is **Article 6(1)(f) GDPR — legitimate interests**, recorded in each [[GDPR Article 30 - ROPA|ROPA]]. Special category data requires an additional Article 9 basis and the Governor's prior approval.
