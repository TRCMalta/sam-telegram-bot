# Firefish

## What it is (plain English)

A recruitment CRM. TRC reads candidate, job, and placement records from Firefish over its API.

## How TRC uses it

Sam queries Firefish to surface candidate pipelines and recent placements when Beverly asks recruiting-related questions.

## Why this matters to auditors

Firefish holds personal data about job candidates. The audit must confirm consent, retention, and whether read-only access is enough (no writes).
