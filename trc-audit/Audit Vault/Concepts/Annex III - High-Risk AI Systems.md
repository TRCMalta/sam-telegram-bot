# Annex III - High-Risk AI Systems

## What it is (plain English)
Annex III of the EU AI Act lists the use cases that are treated as high-risk under Article 6(2). The list is organised into areas including biometrics, critical infrastructure, education, employment, access to essential services, law enforcement, migration, and administration of justice. Annex III §4 specifically covers AI used in employment, worker management, and access to self-employment — for example CV-screening, candidate ranking, task allocation, and performance evaluation tools.

## What it means for TRC
TRC's recruitment activity is the obvious exposure: any AI feature inside Firefish, Odoo, or a bolt-on tool that ranks, scores, or filters candidates is potentially Annex III §4 high-risk. Each such feature triggers the [[Article 26 - Deployer Obligations]] duties. The audit will expect an inventory of recruitment tooling with a per-feature classification (high-risk yes / no / unclear) signed off by the [[DPO]].

## When it applies
The high-risk regime, including Annex III, applies from 2 August 2026. The classification work should be done well in advance so the right contracts and oversight processes are in place.
