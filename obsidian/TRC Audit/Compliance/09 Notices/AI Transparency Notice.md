# AI Transparency Notice

Required by [[Article 50 - AI Transparency|AI Act Article 50]] from **2 August 2026** — every person interacting with an AI system must be told, at the time of first interaction, that they are interacting with AI.

## Where to embed

| Surface | Wording |
|---|---|
| Telegram bot description | _"Sam is an AI assistant operated by TRC. Replies are generated by AI (Claude, by Anthropic). Do not send special-category personal data."_ |
| WhatsApp business profile | Same as above. |
| Sam's first message in any new chat or thread | _"Hi — I'm Sam, an AI assistant. Replies are generated by Claude. I'm operated by TRC. You can ask for a human at any time."_ |
| Sam's first message after a 30-day inactivity gap | Repeat the disclosure. |

## Technical requirements

- Disclosure appears at the **first interaction**, before any other content.
- Wording is in **clear, plain language** in the user's apparent language (English by default).
- Persons with disabilities can perceive the notice — Telegram and WhatsApp both render the text-based notice accessibly.

## Implementation owner

Jonathan (Governor). Implementation deadline **2 August 2026** — no later.
