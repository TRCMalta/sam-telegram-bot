# Worker Notification — Use of High-Risk AI

Required by [[Article 26 - Deployer Obligations|AI Act Article 26(7)]] from **2 August 2026**: before a high-risk AI system is put into service in the workplace, the deployer must inform the affected workers (and their representatives, where applicable) that they will be subject to it.

## Draft text

> **Subject:** TRC use of an AI assistant ("Sam") that reads workplace data
>
> Dear [worker],
>
> The Remarkable Collective ("TRC") operates an AI assistant called **Sam**. Sam helps our Chief Operating Officer, Beverly Cutajar, by answering questions and drafting replies. To do that, Sam reads operational data including the COO's Outlook mailbox, our Odoo HR and CRM records, our Firefish recruitment pipeline, and our Microsoft Teams chats and calendar.
>
> This may include personal data about you. Because TRC operates in the European Union, the EU AI Act (Article 26(7)) requires us to inform you in writing **before** we put this system to use.
>
> What Sam does:
> - Reads the sources listed above on request.
> - Sends the relevant text to **Anthropic's Claude** in the United States to generate replies, under Standard Contractual Clauses.
> - Returns the reply to the COO on Telegram or WhatsApp.
>
> What Sam does **not** do:
> - Take any decision about your employment on its own. A human (the COO or another manager) makes every decision; Sam only assists.
> - Send your data to anyone outside the processors named above.
>
> Your rights:
> - You can ask us what personal data we hold about you and how Sam used it. See [TRC privacy notice].
> - You can object to processing based on legitimate interests (GDPR Art. 21).
> - If you have a concern, you can contact the Governor at jonathan@theremarkablecollective.com or the Maltese Information and Data Protection Commissioner (IDPC) at idpc.info@idpc.org.mt.
>
> Signed,
> Jonathan, Governor
> The Remarkable Collective

## Distribution

- Beverly Cutajar (primary user).
- Every worker whose mailbox, calendar, Teams, Odoo HR or Firefish record can be read by Sam.
