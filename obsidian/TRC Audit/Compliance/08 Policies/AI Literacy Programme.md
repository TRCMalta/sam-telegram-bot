# AI Literacy Programme

Required by [[Article 4 - AI Literacy|AI Act Article 4]] — **already in force since 2 February 2025**.

## Scope

Every TRC worker who **uses, oversees, or commissions** an AI system on TRC's behalf must complete this programme. Currently that includes at minimum:

- Jonathan (Governor — operates [[Judge Dredd]], commissions Sam)
- Beverly Cutajar (COO — primary user of Sam)
- Any future TRC operator of an AI system

The programme is **proportional** to each person's role, prior experience, and the risk class of the AI systems they use. For Sam — likely Annex III §4 high-risk — training depth must be higher.

## Required knowledge (minimum)

1. **What AI is and is not.** Strengths and limitations of generative AI. Hallucination, drift, prompt injection.
2. **TRC's specific AI inventory.** Which agents we run, who they read data from, who owns each (see [[Master Audit]] § 2).
3. **Risk awareness.**
   - Why Sam's use of [[Firefish]] / [[Odoo]] data places it in Annex III §4.
   - The difference between **provider** and **deployer** ([[Article 26 - Deployer Obligations|Art. 26]]).
   - Transparency duty ([[Article 50 - AI Transparency|Art. 50]]) — staff must never disguise AI as a human reply.
4. **Data protection touchpoints.** What counts as personal data, why messages to Sam can carry it, how the [[Sam Chief of Staff|ROPA]] documents lawful basis.
5. **Incident detection.** What to look for, how to report (see `../07 Incidents/Procedure.md`).
6. **Practical do / don't list** for prompting Sam.

## Training records

Stored in `../Evidence/AI Literacy/`. Each record contains:

- Name of trained worker
- Date of completion
- Materials used (curriculum version)
- Test or attestation result
- Next refresh date (annual)

## Refresh trigger

- Annual.
- On material change to the AI inventory.
- On an incident attributed to literacy gap.
