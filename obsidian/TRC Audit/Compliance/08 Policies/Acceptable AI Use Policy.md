# Acceptable AI Use Policy

Applies to every TRC worker, contractor, and authorised user of a TRC AI system.

## Permitted use

- Use TRC-provided AI systems (Sam, [[Judge Dredd]]) for work tasks within the scope each system is designed for.
- Forward operational data that the system already has authorised access to.

## Prohibited use

- Sending **[[Special Category Data]]** (health, trade-union membership, sexual orientation, religious belief, biometric or genetic data) to any AI system without a documented lawful basis and the Governor's approval.
- Sending another organisation's confidential information that is not covered by an existing contract.
- Using TRC AI to take a **fully automated decision producing legal or similarly significant effect** on an individual without the safeguards of GDPR Art. 22.
- Pretending an AI-generated reply was written by a human (breaches [[Article 50 - AI Transparency|AI Act Art. 50]] from 2 August 2026).
- Using TRC credentials with a non-approved AI tool.

## Worker responsibilities

- Complete the [[AI Literacy Programme]] before first use and annually.
- Report incidents (`../07 Incidents/Procedure.md`).
- Treat all output as **draft** — verify before acting on it.

## Enforcement

Material breach is a disciplinary matter under TRC's standard process. Repeated or wilful breach can lead to removal of AI access and contract termination.
