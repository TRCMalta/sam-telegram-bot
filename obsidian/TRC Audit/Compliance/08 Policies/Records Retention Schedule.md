# Records Retention Schedule

| Record category | Retention | Source / basis |
|---|---|---|
| Sam conversation logs (Railway) | **≥ 6 months** | AI Act Art. 26(6) — minimum for high-risk AI |
| Sam conversation logs (Anthropic side) | per Anthropic contractual setting — target **zero retention** | TRC processor agreement |
| Telegram / WhatsApp message metadata | as long as the messaging platform retains it; TRC does not duplicate | platform terms |
| Audit artefacts (this vault) | **6 years** after retirement of the related asset | accountability principle, Maltese statute of limitation |
| DPIAs and ROPAs | for the life of the activity + **6 years** | accountability + supervisory review |
| Personal-data-breach records | **6 years** after closure | GDPR Art. 33(5) implies durable record |
| Data-subject-rights requests | **2 years** after closure | proportionate; longer if litigation reasonably anticipated |
| AI literacy training records | for the duration of employment + **2 years** | evidence of compliance with AI Act Art. 4 |
| Vendor DPAs | for the life of the contract + **6 years** | dispute defence |

The schedule is reviewed annually. Retention longer than the figure above requires Governor approval and a documented lawful basis.
