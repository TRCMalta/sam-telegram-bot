# Article 26 - Deployer Obligations

## What it is (plain English)
Article 26 of the EU AI Act lists the duties of any organisation that deploys a high-risk AI system (as opposed to the provider that builds it). Deployers must use the system according to its instructions, assign human oversight to competent staff, monitor its operation, keep logs, and inform affected people when the system is used on them. If a deployer detects a serious incident or risk, it must notify the provider and the regulator.

## What it means for TRC
Where TRC uses a third-party AI tool that falls under [[Annex III - High-Risk AI Systems]] (most plausibly recruitment or HR scoring tools), TRC becomes a deployer and inherits these duties. The audit will look for: a register of high-risk systems in use, named human overseers, retained logs, and a process to inform candidates or employees. Vendor contracts (Anthropic, Microsoft, Odoo, Firefish) must be reviewed to confirm whether any module triggers high-risk status.

## When it applies
Applies from 2 August 2026. TRC should have its high-risk register and oversight assignments ready before that date.
